Future Plans

I plan to keep the WRX as is. It's a good daily driver, and I feel like adding more power will just lead to more money. This model is known for its glass transmission, and Subaru doesn't have the best track record with ringlands or headgaskets either. Maybe an intake for cool turbo noises, but that's as far as i'll go.

The SV is a great bike, and really my only complaint is the suspension. Due to having the ABS model, im a bit limited on rear shock upgrades, and replacing the front with a GSXR fork would probably leave me with no ABS. May install some valve emulators in the future.
