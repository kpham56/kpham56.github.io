Kevin's Bike Repair

Before and after of a bike I built for my girlfriend. *Not pictured, new vbrakes, levers and wires

Ever since shelter in place began, I found myself biking a lot more. With all this extra free time, I figured might as well explore the Bay Area while getting some fitness out of it. After finding a free bike frame by the garbage, I began fixing it up with used parts from Craigslist. This led to a new obsession, and I found myself looking for bikes to ride and to fix. I began mountain biking, dirt jumping, tandem biking with my mother. Looking for someone to hit up dirt jumps and soquel demo forest with. :)

Repairs I've done

  • Replace rear derailleur, adjust H,L and B screws to perfect shifting
  • Fix and sell a fixed gear bicycle from the frame up
  • Fix, repaint, and sell a single speed mountain bike
  • Fix and repaint commuter bike as seen above, replacing the grips, seat, levers, and more
  • Refill tubeless sealant
  • Adjust pads in both mechanical and hydraulic disc brakes
  • Convert old tandem bike to single speed
