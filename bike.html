Kevin's Bicycle Repair

Ever since shelter in place began, I found myself biking a lot more. With all this extra free time, I figured might as well explore the Bay Area while getting some fitness out of it. After finding a free bike frame by the garbage, I began fixing the bike up with used parts from Craigslist. This led to a new obsession, and I found myself looking for bikes to ride and to fix. I began mountain biking, dirt jumping, even tandem biking with my mother.
